### Lab Meetings
Anyone is welcome to attend our lab meetings, which are currently being held in-person (zoom available). See the [lab calendar](http://roboticexplorationlab.org/calendar.html) for details.

### Prospective Graduate Students
If you are interested in working in our lab, please apply to one of the [graduate programs offered by MIT AeroAstro](https://aeroastro.mit.edu/education/graduate-admission/). Please contact Zac after you have been admitted.

### MIT Graduate Students
If you have been admitted to MIT and are interested in joining our lab, send Zac an [email](mailto:zacm@mit.edu) with your CV and a short description of your background and interests. Also, feel free to join us for a group meeting at any time.

### MIT Undergraduate Students
We often have opportunities for undergraduates to get involved in our research. If you're interested in robots, spacecraft, or control systems, send Zac an [email](mailto:zacm@mit.edu) or join us for a group meeting.

### Visiting Student Researchers
We ocasionally have openings for visiting Masters or PhD students. Undergraduates looking for summer research positions are highly encouraged to apply to MIT's [Summer Research Program](https://oge.mit.edu/msrp/)
